EXHIBTION

 ESSAY

THE SHOW EXHIBITS AND EXPANDS ON FOUR TYPES OF COLLECTIONS: PHOTOGRAPHIC, METAPHORIC, ACOUSTIC AND PHYSICAL.

I printed the Global Room in parts, that is made out of surroundings, ceilings and floors. Each print out is underlined by a shelf that carries a collection. The first shelf carries a collection of jars of sand. I asked few friends of mine to gather a soil sample of where they reside and send it to me. The second shelf displays five plugs that are abstracted by 3D printing at 200%. The third one holds an iPad that displays an interactive website of the global party.

This project was installed with the help of the Lebanese community of Baltimore City.
